Utility-Led Aggregation (ULA)

"ULA" is an innovative initiative under the PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana, designed to empower residential users by facilitating the installation of rooftop solar panels through utility partnerships. This program allows households to access a 1 KW solar system with minimal initial investment. The utility takes charge of the installation, ownership, and maintenance of the system, offering users a subscription model that includes power access. The model exemplifies a utility-driven strategy, ensuring that the solar infrastructure is managed efficiently on behalf of the consumers.
